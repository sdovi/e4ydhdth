HOLLYWOOD Oasis Home MMT is a new large-scale resort complex, located on the sea front in Morphou Bay in the Gaziveren area of North Cyprus. Indeed, has an ideal location, in a sparsely populated, ecologically impeccable place, which is one of the reasons why many people wishing to buy property in Cyprus, decided to buy it here. The complex is located right by the beautiful sandy beach, a short walk from the center of Gaziveren. Gaziveren is a picturesque town in the northern part of the island, where you can find many stores, cafes and restaurants. You can also buy everything you need for your daily needs in the nearby town of Lefke.

Our company has been implementing projects in resort and urban areas for 19 years and has a portfolio of more than 30 completed projects in Turkey. We carefully study each location, finishing material, the latest technological solutions and achievements of science to create a comfortable and safe space for guests, respond to the smallest comments and nuances for uncompromising quality and living happy moments of life of our valued customers. This premium project includes 3 15-storey bright apartment buildings with apartments of different layouts and areas with spacious terraces. All apartments are built with high quality materials and well planned. The complex has an impressive recreation area with 9 large swimming pools and a beautiful garden, spacious sunny areas with sun loungers, as well as a restaurant and a cozy bar. From here you can enjoy breathtaking views of the Mediterranean Sea. The perfect place to enjoy a cocktail or coffee. The complex also has a fitness center, spa and its own water park.
