    Why   Turkey ?

    Today, the dream of a house by the sea is becoming a beautiful reality for many. And why not? After all, real estate in Turkey and Cyprus has long been a competition to apartments in Spain or Italy – housing is cheaper, service – higher, ease of paperwork. Everyone can pick up a place to his taste: whether it is a luxury villa in the mountains or a cozy studio, housing from the developer at the construction stage or secondary. Oasis Home Holding has everything.

    Why  North Cyprus?

    Did you know that:
    Northern Cyprus has been recognized as the number 1 country for investment by Forbes in 2021.
    The successfully developing part of the island impresses with its variety of real estate, pleasant prices, warm clear sea and stunning nature.

    North Cyprus is a place for everyone: from pupils and students, young people dreaming of development and retirees wishing for a private old age. The so-called “Dubai of the 90s” surprises with its large-scale development of infrastructure and quality of construction. Here everything is created for experienced and novice investors in international real estate.
