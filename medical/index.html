Medical tourism

In recent years, medicine in Turkey has become one of the best among European countries. Health care development is a priority for the government of the country. Serious sums are allocated for it – up to 10% of GDP, attention is paid to advanced technical equipment of medical institutions, qualification and accreditation of medical staff is strictly controlled.

With the highest possible quality of treatment and service in Turkish clinics, the prices for medical services are significantly lower than in European institutions, which cannot but attract patients from all over the world.
